Our Story

Founded in 2019 in the heart of Noida, CTRL Media began with a simple mission: to help businesses take control of their digital presence and achieve sustainable growth in the digital landscape.

What started as a small team of passionate digital marketers has now grown into one of the most trusted digital marketing agencies in Delhi NCR, serving clients across India and beyond.

Our name "CTRL Media" represents our core philosophy - giving businesses complete control over their digital growth through strategic planning, creative execution, and data-driven results.
